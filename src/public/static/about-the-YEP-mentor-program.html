Mentee Benefits

The YEP Mentorship Program is designed to provide one-on-one mentoring between college juniors and seniors and current Young Erie Professionals. YEP brings together a variety of mentors representing over a dozen different industries across Erie. The mentorship program is designed to give real-world, hands on experience to mentees. Mentees will have opportunities to collaborate and discuss one-on-one with their mentors. Additional benefits include:

  • Notice of employment opportunities and access to businesses in and outside of their specialty
  • Development of leadership skills
  • YEP Mentorship Program certification of completion
  • Access to YEP resources and the YEP employer network

YEP Mentorship Program Mission

Provide Erie County college/university juniors and seniors with access to a high-level mentoring program that showcases the Young Erie Professionals (YEP) as a means to create relationships between the businesses of Erie County and potential employees, and to increase involvement with YEP and to enhance the satisfaction of membership within YEP

Young Erie Professionals

Young Erie Professionals is dedicated to providing opportunities for emerging leaders and career-minded peers to develop professionally, while actively participating in their community to connect, attract and retain young talent in the local workforce.
