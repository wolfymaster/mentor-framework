Eight months of intense training

There will be 8 months total of the mentorship program for the 2018-2019 school year from September – April. Each month will be given a specific theme and corresponding areas of focus for mentor/mentee discussions for that month. Each month will conclude with some sort of event / recap for that month’s theme. Each month mentors will be provided with suggested activities that will reinforce the monthly theme and a specific area of focus

September :: Spiritual

Part of personal growth is exploring and understanding oneself. In order to set a foundation upon which we will build upon throughout the mentorship program, we start off with a month dedicated to understanding who we are as individuals.
  • Developing your personal why
  • Setting personal SMART goals
  • Gratitude and personal happiness
  • Spirituality

October :: Social

Building Interpersonal relationships and handling conflict. This month will focus on networking, cold introductions, maintaining professional relationships and handling conflict that may arise between coworkers or customers.
  • Building new relationships
  • Developing confidence

November :: Emotional Intelligence

Emotional Intelligence provides empathy necessary to fully understand another’s perspective even when it contradicts one’s own. Recognizing your own thought processes, emotions, and biases can help you make more well-rounded decisions
  • Stess related to work/life balance
  • DISC Personality Analysis
  • Empathy
  • Leadership / Management
